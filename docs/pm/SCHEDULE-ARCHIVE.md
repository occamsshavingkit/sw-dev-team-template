## Usage

- Append historical rows; do not rewrite archival history except to correct factual errors with a dated note.
- Preserve source IDs, dates, status, and reconciliation notes when moving rows from `docs/pm/SCHEDULE.md`.
- Keep acceptance evidence in `docs/pm/SCHEDULE-EVIDENCE.md` unless it is also a historical schedule row.
