# Inventory — `docs/library/` (researcher-owned general standards library)

This is the project's **general standards library**, maintained by
`researcher`. It is **not** a domain SME inventory — domain-specific
external material lives under `docs/sme/<domain>/local/` with its own
`INVENTORY.md`. Items here are cross-cutting, role-agnostic references
(PMBOK, SWEBOK, ISO/IEC/IEEE, ISTQB syllabi, SFIA, etc.) that any agent
may be pointed at.

Shape of the rows below follows `docs/sme/INVENTORY-template.md` with
library-specific fields added (acquisition route, read-restriction
status, paraphrase-policy reminder).

---

## IP policy (project-wide, non-negotiable)

See `CLAUDE.md` § "IP policy (non-negotiable)" and
`docs/glossary/ENGINEERING.md` § "Intellectual property" for binding
definitions.

- **External material is copyrighted by default.** All items below are
  third-party publications. They live in `docs/library/local/` (which
  is gitignored) and MUST NOT be committed.
- **Paraphrase policy.** Agents may paraphrase substantively and cite
  by row ID (e.g., "per LIB-0001 ch. 3"). Do NOT commit verbatim
  excerpts or extended quotations. Prefer paraphrase over quotation
  from standards docs (CLAUDE.md Hard rule #5).
- **No redistribution.** Do not attach, email, or copy these PDFs
  outside `docs/library/local/`. Anyone needing their own copy
  follows the "How to obtain" column.
