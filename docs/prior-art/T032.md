## 5. Known pitfalls

- Silent downgrade: a fallback from frontier/strong to a weaker class needs a recorded reason and reviewable acceptance when task risk is high.
- Authority substitution: `actual_model` must not imply a different role owner or output format.
- Escalation loops: repeated failed fallback should return to `tech-lead` for routing rather than continuing arbitrary provider swaps.
- Safety/security dilution: safety, security, privacy, API, data-model, and release-blocking work must not be downgraded without explicit rationale.

## 6. Recommendation to `architect` / `software-engineer`

Add a fallback section to `docs/model-routing-guidelines.md` that treats provider/model fallback as execution substitution only, requires escalation to frontier/`xhigh` for unresolved conflicts and high-impact boundaries, and blocks silent downgrades that would weaken the owning role's authority or review obligations.
